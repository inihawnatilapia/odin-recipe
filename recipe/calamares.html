Calamares Recipe

Description

Calamares is a popular Filipino and Spanish-inspired dish made of squid rings coated in a light, crispy batter and deep-fried until golden brown. It is typically served with a dipping sauce like aioli, garlic mayo, or spiced vinegar. Crunchy on the outside and tender inside, calamares is a favorite appetizer or pulutan (beer snack). ðŸ¦‘âœ¨

Ingredients

  • 500g squid (calamari)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Lemon wedges
  • Mayonnaise with garlic
  • Spiced vinegar

Steps

  1. Prepare the squid
  2. Season the squid
  3. Prepare the coating
  4. Coat the squid
  5. Heat the oil
  6. Fry in batches
  7. Drain excess oil
  8. Serve and enjoy
