Seafood Meat Vegan

Ingredients:

  • 500g squid (calamari)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Lemon wedges
  • Mayonnaise with garlic
  • Spiced vinegar

Steps:

  1. Prepare the squid
  2. Season the squid
  3. Prepare the coating
  4. Coat the squid
  5. Heat the oil
  6. Fry in batches
  7. Drain excess oil
  8. Serve and enjoy
