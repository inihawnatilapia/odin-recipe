Paksiw na Bangus Recipe

Description

Paksiw na Bangus is a Filipino dish where milkfish (bangus) is simmered in vinegar, garlic, ginger, onions, and peppercorns, often with vegetables like eggplant or ampalaya (bitter melon). The dish has a tangy, slightly salty, and savory flavor, with the vinegar acting as both a seasoning and preservative. Ingredients

  • 1 whole bangus (milkfish)
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon whole black peppercorns
  • 2 pieces eggplant, sliced (optional)
  • 1 ampalaya (bitter melon), sliced (optional)
  • 2‚Äì3 pieces green chili (siling haba) (optional, for spice)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the fish
  2. Arrange the ingredients in a pot
  3. Add seasonings
  4. Bring to a boil
  5. Simmer until cooked
  6. Taste and adjust seasoning
  7. Serve and enjoy
