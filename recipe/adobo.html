Adobo Recipe

Description

Adobo is a popular Filipino dish made by simmering meat (usually chicken or pork) in a savory, tangy, and slightly sweet sauce made f soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and black peppercorns. It is known for its rich flavor and tender texture, often served with rice.

Ingredients

  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Bay leaves
  • Black peppercorns
  • Water
