Tempura Recipe

Description

Tempura is a Japanese dish consisting of seafood or vegetables coated in a light, airy batter and deep-fried until golden and crispy. It is known for its delicate crunch and is often served with a dipping sauce, grated daikon, and steamed rice or noodles. Shrimp tempura is the most popular variety, but other ingredients like sweet potatoes, eggplant, and mushrooms are also commonly used. üç§‚ú®

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup ice-cold water
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Shrimp
  • Vegetables
  • Oil for frying
  • 1/2 cup dashi stock
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons mirin
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Grated daikon radish

Steps

  1. Prepare the ingredients
  2. Make small cuts on the shrimp
  3. Chill the ingredients
  4. Make the batter
  5. Heat the oil
  6. Coat and fry
  7. Drain excess oil
  8. Prepare the dipping sauce
  9. Serve and enjoy
