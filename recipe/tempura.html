Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup ice-cold water
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Shrimp
  • Vegetables
  • Oil for frying
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Grated daikon radish

Steps:

  1. Prepare the ingredients
  2. Make small cuts on the shrimp
  3. Chill the ingredients
  4. Make the batter
  5. Heat the oil
  6. Coat and fry
  7. Drain excess oil
  8. Prepare the dipping sauce
  9. Enjoy!
